About

The Scottish Rite is a Masonic organization and branch of Freemasonry. Freemasonry is the oldest fraternal organization in existence. Our belief is that each man has a responsibility to help make the world a better place. The Scottish Rite of freemasonry is a respected, recognized, and valued fraternal organization. Through our culture of philanthropy, we make a profound difference for our brothers, families, communities, and our future.

Friends & Family Charity Dinner

The Scottish Rite's RiteCareÂ® raises money for thousands of children affected by speech and language disorders. Our RiteCare Childhood Language Disorders Program has been helping children receive treatment and communicate effectively since 1953. The program provides diagnostic evaluation & treatment of speech & language disorders, and learning disabilities at 178 clinics, centers, and special programs throughout the United States.

Have a good time while supporting your community at our Friends & Family Charity Dinner! Meet the people spearheading these efforts, and those affected by our programs. All proceeds are donated to our program to help children affected by speech & learning disorders lead enriched lives. Sponsorships at our dinner are 100% tax deductible. If you cannot make it to this event, we urge you to donate to this important cause. Your donations, sponsorships, and generosity provide a voice to those who cannot speak.
